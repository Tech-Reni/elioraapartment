Eliora Apartment is a premium residential property located in the heart of Waterview Experience Estate, one of Lagos's most sought-after neighborhoods. This exquisite apartment offers the perfect blend of modern luxury, comfort, and convenience.

Featuring 3 spacious bedrooms, 3 modern bathrooms, a fully equipped kitchen, and a stunning living room with breathtaking views, Eliora is designed for those who appreciate the finer things in life.
